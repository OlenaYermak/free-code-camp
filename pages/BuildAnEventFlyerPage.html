Summer City Street Food & Music Festival

Join us for a vibrant summer event — an open-air street food and live music festival! Enjoy gastronomic stands from Kyiv’s best food trucks, live performances by Ukrainian music bands, workshops for kids and adults, chill zones, and diverse food courts.

Date and Time: July 20, 2025, 2:00 PM — 11:00 PM

Venue/Location: Shevchenko Culture and Recreation Park, Kyiv

Participants / Speakers / Invited Guests:

  • Band "Bez Obmezhen"
  • DJ Polyana
  • Band "Malvy"
  • Food trucks: Burger & Co, Tacos Fiesta, Vegan Way, Ice Cream Bar

Event Program / Activities:

  • 2:00 PM — Festival opening, food courts launch
  • 3:00 PM — Children’s workshops
  • 4:00 PM — Live music performances
  • 6:00 PM — DJ set
  • 9:00 PM — Headliner concert by "Bez Obmezhen"
  • 11:00 PM — Festival closing
