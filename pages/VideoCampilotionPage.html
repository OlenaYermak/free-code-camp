How to Care for Popular Flowers

This page offers beginner-friendly tips for taking care of the most beloved flowers: roses, lavender, and orchids. Each section includes a brief explanation and a tutorial video.

Roses

Roses require regular pruning, proper feeding, and moderate watering. This video shows how to take care of them throughout the growing season.

Lavender

Lavender loves sunny spots and well-drained soil. Learn how to prune it and preserve its fragrant flowers in this tutorial.

Orchids

Orchids need special care including proper humidity, lighting, and fertilization. This video explains common mistakes and how to avoid them.
